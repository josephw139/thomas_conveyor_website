Industries

Home » Industries

Thomas Conveyor Company specializes in the design and manufacturing of custom conveyor systems tailored to meet specific applications across diverse industries. With over 60 years of experience, our track record of satisfying customers provides us with a distinctive advantage in addressing and surpassing your bulk material handling conveyor requirements.

Among the industries we have catered to are aggregate, agriculture, minerals & chemical mining/processing, food & beverage processing/handling, building/construction, lumber/wood, utilities, recycling, oil/gas, and wastewater treatment.

Thomas Conveyor Company works with virtually any industry. Thomas can improve system efficiencies in heavy duty / abrasive environments, eliminate corrosion failures when conveying the most highly caustic products, and avoid catastrophic failures in those most challenging system applications. The industries we serve benefit from many of our unique custom equipment features such as:

  • Self-cleaning systems
  • Wear-resistant components
  • Watertight or waterproof systems
  • Systems that repel tacky material build-up on conveying components
  • Components with quick replacement features

Our custom equipment, engineering and design assistance, problem-solving products and innovations, and our stock availability and service levels will exceed your expectation in whatever your industry.
